Platinum Hotel

Our Hotel in Edinburgh Centre puts you 0.5 mi (0.8 km) from Royal Mile and 0.6 mi (0.9 km) from Edinburgh Castle. Guests can take a dip in the indoor pool or grab a bite to eat at The TAP Bar & Kitchen, which serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. This upmarket hotel also features a bar/lounge, a fitness centre and a sauna. Fellow travellers say great things about the central location and comfortable guest rooms.
